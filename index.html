Carbon Neutrality

Reducing GHG emissions across the full vehicle lifecycle: manufacturing, logistics, and product. With a 2035 target for carbon neutrality at all North American facilities and 70% electrified new vehicle sales in the US by 2030.

2035 target: Carbon neutrality at all North American facilities
Explore targets + data →

Circular Economy

Keeping materials in use and out of landfills, ensuring today's resources become the building blocks for tomorrow's mobility. From closed-loop aluminum recycling to battery material recovery.

Target: 90%+ battery material recovery at TBMNC
Explore stories →

Nature Positive

Supporting the protection of water resources, restoring biodiversity, and partnering with communities to strengthen ecosystems. 17 North American sites hold Wildlife Habitat Council certification.

Target: Protect or restore 62,000 acres across North America

The Products Living the Commitment

Sustainability is not only about what happens behind the scenes. It is in every vehicle we make: the technology, the materials, the efficiency, and the electrification choices available to every driver today. In 2025, 47% of all Toyota and Lexus vehicles sold in the US were electrified.
